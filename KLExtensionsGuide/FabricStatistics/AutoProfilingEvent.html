AutoProfilingEvent (struct)¶

The AutoProfilingEvent helper struct simplifies the recording of a profiling event by automatically calling BeginProfilingEvent in its constructor and EndProfilingEvent in its destructor. This is intended to be created as a local variable, whose scope is the same as the profiled event. See Profiling for an example of usage.
