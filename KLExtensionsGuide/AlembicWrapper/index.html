AlembicWrapper Extension¶

Fabric Engine version 2.0.0
Copyright 2010-2015 Fabric Software Inc. All rights reserved.

The AlembicWrapper Extension provides a high level wrapping for the Alembic Extension. Even though this layer introduces abstraction, the low level alembic objects such as the AlembicIObject or AlembicOObject can still be accessed. The main objects of this extension are the AlembicArchiveReader and the AlembicArchiveWriter.
